A little more about the role

As an Early Childhood Educator, you will work closely with the Educational Leader to provide high-quality education and care for children accessing the service. Programs are informed by the Belonging, Being and Becoming: Early Years Learning Framework and reflect current best practice while supporting the individual needs of each child.

Plan, implement and evaluate developmentally appropriate education programs that:

Respond to the strengths and interests of each child

Reflect the philosophy of the service and Children’s Services

Align with the Early Years Learning Framework and National Quality Standards

Include indoor and outdoor learning with a focus on education for sustainability

Build nurturing, respectful relationships with children that recognise their rights, differences and choices

Provide supervision and personal care while maintaining safe, welcoming environments

Lead and support other educators as part of a collaborative team

Document learning, observations and required reports in accordance with CoM policies and regulatory requirements

Actively contribute to the Quality Improvement Plan and continuous improvement of the service

Develop positive partnerships with families and encourage their engagement in the centre community

Liaise with community services and other City of Melbourne teams to support children and families

Support and supervise students on placement

Participate in professional learning, staff meetings and professional conversations

Maintain compliance with OHS requirements, emergency management procedures and a workplace culture free from bullying, discrimination and harassment
